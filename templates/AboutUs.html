What our website is all about?

Our website outlines the progress of the 'EEG-based Cognitive Assessment Website' project, which aims to develop a non-invasive, real-time cognitive health monitoring platform using EEG technology and machine learning algorithms. The platform is designed to address the challenges of early detection and intervention in neurodegenerative diseases such as Alzheimer's and Parkinson's diseases. The system uses EEG data to provide users with personalised cognitive assessment, health tracking, risk analysis and preventive advice. Key advances include the development of a website prototype, database architecture, feature extraction methods, and initial AI model training for Alzheimer's disease detection. Challenges such as data scarcity, real-time processing and user comfort have been identified and mitigation strategies proposed.
